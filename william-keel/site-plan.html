Content

Home page

Intro The people who will be visiting this site will hopefully be people looking to learn about my skills and abilities as a web/software developer as part of the investigation/interview process. The people who come to this site will need to know who I am, what skills I have, my resume, why they should/shouldn’t hire me, and how I would fit in with their current team and work culture. My site will offer the ability to contact me. This won’t be completed with this assignment but it is something I will work on afterwards. Therefore, the contact me section will be a small part of my website and a design for now. Main The main page of this site is to very briefly introduce the readers to me and this site. There won’t be too much information on this page in the form of text because I don’t want people to get distracted by the focus of the site which is to learn about me. On this site I will have links to my git repository, my resume (page 2), and my about me page (page 3). Ultimately this page will have to have 2 images per the requirements. The images will be a sketch of a picture of me (in black and white of course) and my logo. I know this might not meet the requirements of 2-3 images but I am really going for a minimalist thing here and a resume page doesn’t really need lots of pictures. Obviously, in the header of each page I am going to have my logo, which is just my initials, and the nav bar. In the footer I will have social media links and icons.
